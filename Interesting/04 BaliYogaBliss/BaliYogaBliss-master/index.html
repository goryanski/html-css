Our space

Experience a journey of a lifetime and the best Bali has to offer as a Yoga Retreat at the very south-coast of Bali located at a hill-top 5-Star resort overlooking the Melasti Beach area. The entire territory of the complex is a real garden of paradise, with colorful flowers that attract bees and butterflies, mango trees and romantic alleys. You will have an amazing experience living in a fabulous luxurious space that ideally contributes to your transformation!

Beach

You will have an opportunity to swim in the open blue waters, enjoy surfing & a variety of other water sports, watch beautiful sunsets from the beach, visit local markets or just enjoy the sun and relax. At Bali Yoga Bliss we will make your dream yoga vacation come true, where you can recharge your body, uplift your mind, & enlighten your soul with yoga, meditation, philosophical exploration and self-realization.

Bali

Set above the vast blue Bali Ocean … we will help you remember how to cultivate a simple philosophy to help you create a real and lasting change in your life. So you can connect deeply to your spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being

Meditation

A restless mind is the main cause of emotional distress and illness. The body has tremendous power of self-healing from nature, but we block this potential with our negative thoughts and unconscious attitudes. Thanks to meditation, you can better control your thoughts and actions, clear your head “of debris” and find out what you really need. The body will relax more and you will get rid of muscle clamps, the spine will fall into place, the organs will begin to work better, fatigue will go away, sleep and well-being will improve, you will stop worrying and start living in the moment of here and now!
